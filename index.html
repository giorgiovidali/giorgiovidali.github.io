Introduction

This is the "Journal" of Marcus Severus, my latest character in the Role Playing Game "Skyrim". A simple project that allows me to combine a handful of my hobbies: role-playing, video-games, fantasy writing, and some coding just for kicks. As of now, I am more interested in the development of the character Marcus Severus and am not very much concerned with the technical aspect of assembling a website. In fact, the simple and economical markup is just a way to easily share my stories with anybody who would care to read them. Do not expect much, from the aesthetic point of view.

If you are not familiar with Role-Playing games and video-games, if you do not know much (or anything at all) about the world of the Elder Scrolls and Skyrim, just take a look at the "About Skyrim" section and the backstory of the character Marcus Severus. Or just jump into Marcus Severus' journal entries, in any order but - preferably - just one after the other. I'll keep adding more journal entries as I continue to play the game.

Have fun, if you can tolerate the writing.
